Services

Kayaking

Kayaking involves paddling through the water on a small, narrow boat known as a kayak. It's a great way to explore calm waters, coves, and even venture into mangrove forests.

Jet Skiing

Jet skiing involves riding a personal watercraft at high speeds across the water. Riders can perform exhilarating maneuvers and feel the thrill of the waves as they skim the surface.

Snorkeling

Snorkeling allows you to explore the underwater world while wearing a mask and a snorkel. You can observe colorful fish, coral reefs, and other marine life just below the water's surface.

Windsurfing

Windsurfing combines elements of surfing and sailing, where participants use a board with an attached sail to ride the waves while harnessing the power of the wind.

Water Skiing

Water skiing involves being towed behind a boat while wearing skis and gliding on the water's surface. It requires a bit of balance and coordination but is immensely enjoyable.
